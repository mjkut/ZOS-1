Mbira Music

The Mbira, also known as the thumb piano, is one of Zimbabwe's most iconic traditional instruments. It consists of metal keys attached to a wooden board, and it is traditionally played by the Shona people.

The Mbira is often played during ceremonies, rituals, and celebrations to connect with ancestors.

Amakondere Dance

Amakondere is a traditional dance that originated from the Matabele people, often performed during celebratory events. The dance is characterized by energetic movements and rhythmic drumming.

The dancers often wear colorful costumes and their movements are synchronized with drumming beats, creating an electrifying atmosphere.

Jiti Music and Dance

Jiti is a popular Zimbabwean music genre and dance style that blends traditional rhythms with modern instruments. It is widely enjoyed at social gatherings and is known for its fast tempo and upbeat vibe.

It is often accompanied by lively dancing and socializing, making it an essential part of Zimbabwean celebrations.
