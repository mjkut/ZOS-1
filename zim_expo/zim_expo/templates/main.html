About Zimbabwe Pavilion

The Zimbabwe Pavilion at Expo 2025 Osaka serves as a cultural, economic, and innovation hub representing Zimbabwe on the world stage. Under the theme "Beyond the Limits," the pavilion showcases Zimbabwe's rich heritage, vibrant culture, and emerging opportunities in tourism, trade and investment. The pavilion aims to foster global connections, promote sustainable economic growth and highlight Zimbabwe’s advancements in various sectors.

Mission and Vision

Mission

To present Zimbabwe as a forward-looking, innovative nation that values both its cultural heritage and economic potential. The pavilion seeks to engage visitors in meaningful experiences, share stories of Zimbabwe's growth and encourage international partnerships.

Vision

To establish Zimbabwe as a key player in global tourism, investment and trade by showcasing the unique strengths, resources and opportunities available in the country.
